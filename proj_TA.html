Teaching Experience

While working as a Teaching Assistant for the mechatronics courses my duties included teaching the course content in tutorials, running the labs where students got hand on experience working on hardware and grading submitted coursework. For my teaching in the 2020-2021 year I was recognized with the "Dean's Teaching Assistant Award".

MECH 217 Measurements for Mechatronics

Fall 2019, Fall 2020

As TA for MECH 217 I provided students with assistance on how to use mircrocontrollers for the purpose of measurements and analysis. Students learned to Identify and qantify measurement objectives in practical engineering applications; apply statistical analysis, including uncertainty, for interpreting test results. Specify, select, and apply transducers, acquisition systems, and 2 procedures to measure pressure, strain, temperature and position using both analog and digital sesnsors.

MECH 210 Electronic Circuits and Motors for Mechatronics

Winter 2020, Winter 2021 (Head TA), Winter 2022 (Head TA)

As TA for MECH 210 I taught circuit analysisis techniques of RLC circuits in tutorial sessions. Theses methods are then used to characterize the behavior of common mechatronic circuits; transformers, diodes, solenoids, DC motors and actuators. Students are then introduced to transistors, and their switching applications. The theory is then applied in the lab component of the course which I facilitated, guiding students various methods of DC motor control using microcontrollers and force control of a series elastic actuator.

MECH 310 Digital Systems for Mechatronics

Fall 2022 (Head TA)

As TA for MECH 310 I cover and review course material in tutorial sessions. This covers a wide range of topics including; servo motors, stepper motors PLC control of sequential logic FFT application for machine health monitoring, industrial communication standards for local and internet-based information transfer. The lab sessions which I administered focused on design, construction, and testing of microcontroller based mechatronic systems such as programming a rapid prototyped CNC machine to run g-code to draw the Queen's logo.
