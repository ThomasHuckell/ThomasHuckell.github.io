Kayak Guide & Instructor

While working as a kayak guide and instructor I was able to develop leadership and teaching skills through instructing clients on paddling fundamentals in a range of paddling courses. Additionally, as a senior guide I help with training and mentoring new staff on how to run the tours and lessons that Deep Cove Kayak offers.

When running large group tours and overnight school trips I often work with a team of guides to manage the group, making sure all the clients are together, safe and having a enjoyable experience. To maintain this requires teamwork and communication with the other guides as well as strong incident management and risk assessment when dealing with hazards of coastal environments.

It has been very rewarding being able to share my passion of paddling and the outdoors to others. I am always looking forward to getting out on the water and explore.
