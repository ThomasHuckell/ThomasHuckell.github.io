Get to know me!

I'm a recent MASc graduate in robotics currently living in Vancouver looking to start my career in the robotics industry.

I completed my BASc degree in Applied Mathematics and Mechanical Engineering at Queen's University. This program developed a strong theoretical background in modern controls and modeling of robotic systems.

I completed my MASc degree at Queen's University under the supervision of Amy Wu at Queen's University. My research focused on the standing balance of legged robots.

Outside of work I enjoy being outdoors! I am an avid paddler who enjoys kayaking and stand up paddleboarding. I also have a passion for woodworking , specifically designing and building furnature.
